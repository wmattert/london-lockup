Frequently Asked Questions

When is my payment due?

Payments are due on the 1st of each month. A late fee will be applied after the 5th of the month.

How do I set up automatic payments?

You can set up automatic payments by logging into your account and selecting the "Auto-Pay" option in your account settings.

What if I need to change my payment method?

You can update your payment method at any time by logging into your account and updating your payment information.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, all payment information is securely processed through Clover, a PCI-compliant payment processor. London LockUp does not store your card details.
